University Halls

Undergraduate students

University of Edinburgh offers a variety of options for undergraduate students. Read full undergraduate guide here.

Room guarantee

  • For Freshers
    • The University of Edinburgh is your first choice and you are not applying to the Univeristy on a Conditional Insurance basis
    • Application is recieved by 16 August
    • Your normally reside outside of Edinburgh
    • You are studying at the University of Edinburgh for the whole academic year starting in September
  • Conditional Insurance students with another university as their No1 choice
    • Guarantee is only for shared accommodation(often with bunk beds)
    • Must still apply for accommodation
  • Visiting undergraduates
    • Application is received by 16 August
    • Accepted by 30 August an unconditional offer
  • A room is not guaranteed for couples and families
    • Limited supply of suitable accommodation
    • Allocated on a first-come first-served basis

Anticipated rents

  • Catered
    • From £6,242 to £8,411 for a 37-week lease
    • Options for shared rooms are available from £4,693 to £5,301
  • Self-catered
    • From £3,427 to £6,064 for a 38-week lease
    • Some options for twin rooms are available from £2,324.
  • Rents include
    • utility bills
    • Personal contents insurance
    • Data network
    • Facilities and support from the Residence Life team
    • Cleaning and housekeeping varies according to the location, but usually includes all common areas, staircases etc.

Postgraduate students

University of Edinburgh offers a variety of options for postgraduate students. Read full postgraduate guide here.

Accommodation guarantee

  • Taught postgrads within the EU (including UK)
    • Not currently guaranteed
    • encouraged to apply anyway
  • Guaranteed
    • all new single postgraduate research (typically PhD) students, whether from the UK, EU or International
    • all new single postgraduate taught students from outside the EU only
    • cannot guarantee to provide everyone with precisely the type of accommodation they would wish.
    • application is received by 31 July 2016;
    • unconditional offer, which you must have firmly accepted, to study at the University by 31 July 2016;
    • will be studying in Edinburgh for the whole of the academic year starting in September
    • contracts from September to September.
  • Applications for accommodation are currently expected to open from early February
  • Offers of accommodation will normally be made from May

Anticipated rents

  • Weekly rents below relate to single rooms in self-catered flats between £83 – £166 per week (51 week lease)
  • University accommodation rents include all utility bills; personal contents insurance; internet facilities and support from the Residence Life team. Cleaning and housekeeping varies according to the location and type of accommodation offered but usually includes all common areas, staircases etc. within our premises.
